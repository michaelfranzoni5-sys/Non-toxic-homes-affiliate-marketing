How We Review Products

🔬 Our Review Process

Every product guide is built on a foundation of research, comparison, and honest assessment. Here's how we do it:

1. Research & Selection

We start by identifying the top products in each category using multiple sources: customer reviews, industry certifications, expert recommendations, and manufacturer specifications. We analyze 15-30 products per category before narrowing to our top 5-7 picks.

2. Criteria Development

For each category, we define the criteria that actually matter. For water filters, that's filtration capability, NSF certifications, flow rate, and filter lifespan. For air purifiers, it's CADR ratings, filter type, room size coverage, and noise levels. We publish these criteria so you know exactly what we're comparing.

3. Comparison & Testing

We compare products side-by-side against our criteria. When possible, we personally test products in real home environments. When we can't test every product, we're transparent about it — and we rely on verified customer data, third-party certifications, and extensive research to fill the gaps.

4. Honest Assessment

Every product we recommend gets a clear pros and cons list. We never hide the downsides. If a product has a flaw, we tell you. We also rate each product on a 1-10 scale so you can compare at a glance.

5. Ongoing Updates

We update our guides regularly — every 3-6 months — to reflect new products, price changes, and new research. Each guide shows its "last updated" date so you know it's current.
