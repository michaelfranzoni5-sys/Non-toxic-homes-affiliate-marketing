## Key Insights for Temple Keep

1. **They're e-commerce + coaching; we're content + affiliate** — Different models, we compete on search + free info
2. **They ignore home infrastructure** — Zero content on water, air, cookware, sleep. We own this completely
3. **Their "guides" are paid** — $ digital downloads behind paywall. We give better info for free → SEO advantage
4. **Their blog exists but is minimal** — They have `/blogs/blog` but no SEO depth
5. **Personal authority vs data authority** — They lead with Caitlin's personal story. We lead with research + data. Both valid, but our approach is more scalable
6. **No longevity angle** — They don't connect non-toxic living to anti-aging / longevity. This is our unique wedge

## Applied Action

Created "Non-Toxic Home Priority Roadmap" — a free, comprehensive cornerstone guide that:
- Answers "Where do I start?" (their paid guide approach, but free + better)
- Establishes a priority framework (Water → Air → Cookware → Sleep → Cleaning → Personal Care)
- Cross-links to all existing Temple Keep articles
- Includes a decision flowchart and priority matrix
- Directly contrasts with nontoxichomes.com's paid-only model
